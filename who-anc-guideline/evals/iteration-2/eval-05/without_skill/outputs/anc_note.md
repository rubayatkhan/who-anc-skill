2. **GDM risk factors identified:**
   - Previous macrosomic baby (4.2 kg)

4. **Hemoglobin is normal** — continue current supplementation.

3. **Exercise** — Encourage regular walking or other moderate activity. Patient is currently sedentary.

4. **Continue current medications** — Iron 60 mg + folic acid 0.4 mg daily.

5. **Fetal monitoring** — Normal movements reported. Continue kick counting.

6. **Follow up** — Return in approximately 4 weeks (around 30 weeks). Review GTT results at that visit. If GDM is confirmed, will need dietary management plan and possibly referral.

7. **Routine counseling** — Danger signs, birth planning, nutrition.
