Counsel patient to present immediately to the facility if any of the following occur:
- Severe headache that does not resolve with rest
- Visual disturbances (blurring, flashing lights, spots)
- Epigastric or right upper quadrant pain
- Vaginal bleeding (any amount)
- Decreased or absent fetal movement
- Sudden swelling of face, hands, or feet
- Fever or chills
- Regular contractions before 37 weeks
- Leaking of fluid from the vagina
