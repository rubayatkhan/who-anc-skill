## Expected Clinical Actions (Evaluation Key)

- Classify as moderate anemia (Hb 9.2 g/dL is in the 7.0-9.9 range)
- In a setting with anemia prevalence >=40%, WHO recommends therapeutic-dose iron: 120 mg elemental iron daily
- Add folic acid 0.4 mg daily
- Provide dietary counseling: increase iron-rich foods, vitamin C with meals, avoid tea/coffee with meals
- Administer deworming (single-dose albendazole or mebendazole) after first trimester
- Recheck hemoglobin in 4 weeks to assess response
- If Hb does not improve or drops below 7 g/dL, refer for further evaluation
- Tetanus: 1 booster dose needed (2 prior doses documented)
- Counsel on danger signs of severe anemia (severe fatigue, breathlessness, palpitations)
- No aspirin indicated (no pre-eclampsia risk factors beyond primigravidity alone, which is a single moderate risk factor)
