## Assessment

### 1. Early Pregnancy — First Trimester
Blessing is a 16-year-old primigravida at approximately 8 weeks gestation. Given her young age and uncertain dates, an ultrasound for pregnancy dating is recommended.

### 2. Low Body Weight
Her BMI of 17.6 is below normal range. She should receive dietary advice to increase caloric intake with a balanced diet including protein, fruits, and vegetables. Given household food insecurity reported by her mother, consider referral to available nutrition support services.

### 3. Mild Anemia
Hemoglobin of 10.5 g/dL indicates mild anemia. Start iron supplementation and folic acid. Recommend iron-rich foods in her diet.

### 4. Mental Health Concerns
The patient screens positive on PHQ-2 (score 4/6) with depressed mood and anhedonia. No suicidal ideation. Given her age, unplanned pregnancy, and social circumstances, she would benefit from counseling and emotional support. Consider follow-up screening with a more comprehensive tool.

### 5. Social Considerations
- Adolescent with unplanned pregnancy
- Concerns about school continuation and social stigma
- Supportive mother present
- Limited household income and food insecurity
